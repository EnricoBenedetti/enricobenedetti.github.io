Hoping to see more in the future. If you live in a more or less remote area of Japan, send me a message and tell me about it!!

When you live in Tokyo, there's a bit of everything within reach.......

Sumida acquarium and Tokyo Skytree
Kappabashi, area where they sell many cooking utensils
Meiji-jingu and gardens
Shinjuku ni-chome ;)
Gardens and boat ride and Hachitama observatory in Odaiba
Akihabara (darts, shops), Ikebukuro, Asakusa, Tokyo station

Hiking in Saitama, reaching the Earth Observation Center and the children's zoo

Yokohama's pier and malls, and big Gundam museum (closing...)

Mitaka and the Ghibli Musem

Mount Takao museum and hike

Stations Fujiyoshida and Shimoyoshida of the Fujikyuko Line

And one of the best excursions
From Kita-Kamakura station, there are buddhist temples; take a hike (Daibutsu Hiking Trail) to the big buddha statue and the beach. Along the way, Kuzuharagaoka Shrine
